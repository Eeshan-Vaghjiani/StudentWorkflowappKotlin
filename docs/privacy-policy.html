Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-11-07

1. Introduction

Welcome to Team Collaboration App. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our mobile application.

By using Team Collaboration App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information when you use our app:

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation, authentication, and communication
  • Password: Stored securely in hashed format for account authentication
  • Profile Pictures: Optional images you upload to personalize your profile
  • Display Name: Your chosen name displayed to other users

2.2 User-Generated Content

  • Messages: Text messages and communications you send within the app
  • Tasks: Task information including titles, descriptions, due dates, and assignments
  • Groups: Group names, memberships, and related metadata

2.3 Technical Information

  • FCM Tokens: Firebase Cloud Messaging tokens used to send push notifications to your device
  • Device Information: Basic device information for app functionality and troubleshooting
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the app to improve our services

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and provide secure access to your account
  • App Functionality: To enable core features including messaging, task management, and group collaboration
  • Push Notifications: To send you notifications about messages, task updates, and group activities
  • Communication: To respond to your inquiries and provide customer support
  • Service Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve app performance and features
  • Security: To detect, prevent, and address technical issues and fraudulent activity

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase services, which provide enterprise-grade security and reliability.

4.1 Storage Services

  • Firebase Authentication: Securely stores your authentication credentials with industry-standard encryption
  • Cloud Firestore: Stores your profile information, messages, tasks, and group data
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Manages push notification tokens and delivery

4.2 Data Location

Your data is stored on Google's secure servers. Firebase services may store data in multiple locations to ensure reliability and performance. For more information about Firebase's data storage practices, please visit Firebase Privacy and Security.

4.3 Security Measures

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Access to user data is restricted and monitored
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed

5. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share your data in the following circumstances:

5.1 Service Providers

We use Google Firebase as our backend service provider. Firebase processes your data on our behalf to provide authentication, database, and messaging services. Firebase is bound by strict data protection agreements and complies with GDPR and other privacy regulations.

5.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities (e.g., a court order or government agency).

5.3 Within the App

Your profile information (display name and profile picture) is visible to other users within groups you join. Messages and tasks you create are visible to members of the relevant groups.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

6.1 Access and Correction

You can access and update your profile information at any time through the app settings.

6.2 Data Deletion

You have the right to request deletion of your account and associated data. To delete your account, visit our Account Deletion Page. Upon deletion, the following data will be removed:

  • Your user profile and authentication credentials
  • Messages you have sent
  • Tasks you have created
  • Your group memberships
  • Your FCM tokens

Please note that some data may be retained in anonymized form for analytics purposes, and messages in group chats may remain visible with your identity removed.

6.3 Data Portability

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used format. Please contact us using the information provided below to request your data.

6.4 Opt-Out of Communications

You can disable push notifications through your device settings or the app settings at any time.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Team Collaboration App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.

If we become aware that we have collected personal information from a child under 13 without verification of parental consent, we will take steps to delete that information as quickly as possible.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately using the contact information below, and we will take appropriate action.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you with our services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except for:

  • Data we are required to retain for legal or regulatory purposes
  • Anonymized data used for analytics and service improvement
  • Backup data, which is automatically deleted after 30 days

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information. Your continued use of the app after any changes indicates your acceptance of the updated policy.

For significant changes that materially affect your rights, we will provide prominent notice through the app or via email.
